About MadeByRobot

MadeByRobot is a sparse-cooperation organization of creative developers, artists, and digital nomads who create and maintain high-quality, user-focused games and apps.

Our mission is to deliver exceptional products that genuinely delight users, leveraging AI while human creativity and taste direct the vision. We operate as independent collaborators united by our passion for quality.

User-Focused

User value is our first and foremost consideration in everything we build

Quality-Driven

We deliver excellence in every product, prioritizing quality over speed

Independent

Each project is autonomous, allowing creative freedom and innovation
